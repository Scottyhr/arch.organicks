Mycelial Insulation

Mycelial insulation begins with the growth of the mycelium in a controlled environment until the mycelium has developed over the substrate. The mixture is then sprayed into cavity walls, where it continues to grow and provide full insulation within 6 months.

Sustainability Impact

Our insulation method helps sequester ~125,000 kg of carbon per year in an average UK residence, making it a powerful tool in the fight against climate change.

Research and Development

We are continuously innovating, with ongoing research into the genetic engineering of mycelium to enhance its insulation properties.
